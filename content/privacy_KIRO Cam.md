KIRO Cam respects your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how KIRO Cam handles information when you use the app.

## No Account Required

KIRO Cam does not require registration or login.

You can use the app without creating an account.

## No Data Collection

KIRO Cam does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

We do not collect:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Location data
- Photos
- Videos
- Device identifiers

## Camera & Photo Library Access

KIRO Cam may request access to:

- Camera
- Photo Library

These permissions are only used for core app functionality, such as taking photos and saving images locally on your device.

Your photos and videos never leave your device.

## No Analytics

KIRO Cam does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

We do not track user activity.

## No In-App Purchases

KIRO Cam currently does not offer:

- Subscriptions
- In-app purchases
- Paid accounts

## Data Security

Since KIRO Cam does not collect or transmit user data, your information remains stored locally on your device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page.
