Welcome to "CheerGlow" App (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user information and the rights of users while using the App.

## 1. Information Collection

The App is an offline app, does not require login, and does not connect to the Internet. Except for voluntary actions by users, the App **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information**.

## 2. In-App Purchases

The App provides a one-time purchase. All payment transactions are processed by Apple's payment system. The App **does not access or store any payment information**.

## 3. Data Storage

All functions of the App are performed locally on the user's device. User data, such as chosen support colors, is stored only on the device.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App currently does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services and does not share user information.

## 5. Policy Updates

If future versions introduce network features or data collection, this Privacy Policy will be updated, and users will be notified within the App.
